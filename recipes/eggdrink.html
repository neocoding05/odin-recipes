Egg Drink

This is honestly just quick and easy protein when you dont feel like eating.

Description

Its a recipe that is suprisingly tasty.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. bring water to a boil
  2. add your 3 eggs for approximately 15-20 seconds
  3. Take them out and add to a glass
  4. Proceed to throw in some soy sauce
  5. Drink !
