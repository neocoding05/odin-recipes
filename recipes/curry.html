Japanese Curry

Description

Japan is a well known country, something lesser known is the amazing curry it has to offer.

This will be the recipe my mother always used to make, sadly not from scratch.

For me, it reminds me of home, a mouthful of love and a belly filled.

It has a savory taste to it and a creamy texture. I hope you'll love the recipe as much as I do.

Ingredients

  • Japanese curry paste
  • Two Potatoes
  • 300 gr Beef
  • Two Carrots
  • Two Onions
  • One Apple
  • Four cloves of Garlic
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Dice your onions, and cut your beef
  2. Dice your carrots into bite sized pieces, skin your potatoes and cube them
  3. Now throw in the onions in a warm pan on medium heat, add the beef after a minute
  4. Cook till the meat is no longer red and throw in 4 cups of water
  5. add your diced onion and carrot to the cooking pot
  6. let simmer for twenty minutes
  7. In the down time, you could prepare your rice
  8. Mince your garlic and rasp your apple, once finished throw it in and stir the pot
  9. Now you have a lovely curry
