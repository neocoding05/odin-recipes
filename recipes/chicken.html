Chicken Dish

The reason why I called this "Chicken Dish", is due to the fact

that I just used to make this when younger and never gave it a name.

The recipe itself is upto interpretation, so you can deviate from the recipe.

Description

Its a recipe with a lot of flavour, nice to add some rice to it.

Its really simple and mostly uses the flavour combination of simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 300 gr Chicken
  • One large onion
  • Two small tomatoes
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Preferred seasoning, including salt
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Chop an onion and start to mince your garlic
  2. Cut your chicken and tomato into pieces
  3. Procceed to throw your onions into a pan on medium heat
  4. Add the garlic while maintaining a stir so nothing burns
  5. After everything looks a little glazed out, you add your tomato
  6. You lower the heat and let it simmer, but first procceed to add your chicken and spices
  7. If you see it doesnt have enough water to simmer, add a little water to it, not too much as it takes away from the taste.
  8. While simmering, you can cook the rice
  9. Now enjoy
